Privacy Policy for Natural History Museum of Mongolia's AR Mobile App

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Natural History Museum of Mongolia's AR mobile app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we do not collect, how we use your data, and our commitment to maintaining the confidentiality and security of your information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Our AR mobile app does not collect any personal data, usage data, or any other information from its users. The app operates entirely offline, meaning it does not require an internet connection to function, and it does not contain any advertisements.

3. Offline Functionality

The app's content, including videos, 3D content, and text information billboards, is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to or from our servers, ensuring a completely offline and private experience.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take prompt action to delete such information from our records.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services or platforms. We do not use any third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or other services that could potentially collect data from our users.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of the app after the posting of any revised Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
